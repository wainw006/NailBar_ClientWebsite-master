    The Nail Bar is an in-service salon concept supplying the ultimate convenience for businesses whose clients seek nail care services. Collaborating with The Beauty Lounge Minneapolis we will have full-time nail technicians providing nail care services equipped with both stationary & mobile stations that will allow hair care clients the opportunity to maximize their appointment time and get their nails done under the dryer! The Nail Bar will position itself by providing an attractive option that will meet our clients at the cross-roads of 'experience', 'convenience', and 'quality'.
